About Me

During my undergradute years, I was fortunate enough to land an internship at Microchip Technology within their Analog Power Architecture and Applications group where I worked part time during school and full time in summers for over 2 years! Upon graduating with my undergraduate degree, I took a full time position at Finger Lakes Instrumentation, a division of the IDEX Health Science LLC. in Binghamton New York while also pursuing my Masters degree. I worked within their life science optics research, development and engineering group focusing on embedded motion control systems development for optical filterwheels and linear stage focusing devices. I currently work at Microchip Technology once again where I work in the USB & Networking Test Development Group supporting software and hardware development for system level testing of new product devices while in an automated production test environment. In my free time, I enjoy being outdoors and doing all sorts of cool activities such as hiking, biking, and kayaking.
